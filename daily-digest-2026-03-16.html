1. Agentic AI & Frameworks

MCP Becomes the Industry Standard: The "USB-C for AI Agents" Is Now Officially Governed by the Agentic AI Foundation

AIMultiple & SoftMaxData — March 2026

Anthropic's Model Context Protocol (MCP) — which provides a standardised way for AI agents to connect to external tools such as databases, search engines, and APIs — has been donated to the newly formed Agentic AI Foundation, now backed by Anthropic, OpenAI, Google, Microsoft, AWS, Block, Cloudflare, and Bloomberg. The move effectively crowns MCP as the universal connector layer for agentic AI, reducing fragmentation across the six major competing frameworks. Analysts describe MCP as having arrived at precisely the right moment: as enterprises scale beyond proof-of-concept agents, the lack of a shared tool-calling standard had become the primary integration bottleneck.

With MCP reducing friction between agents and real enterprise systems, practitioners predict 2026 will be the year agentic workflows finally exit the demo stage and enter day-to-day operations at scale, with Gartner forecasting 40% of enterprise applications will feature task-specific AI agents by year-end, up from under 5% in 2025.
